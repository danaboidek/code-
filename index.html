Girl Scouts

Redesign Girl Scouts website to create a “rebranded” experience that moves away from monetary gains through sales and highlights the social and communal growth which is possible through involvement in the group. A heavy focus on social media study led to a geographic troop locator with leader bios and a streamlined version of the enrollment process.

Rambler

Design of an app which integrates travel and social experience based on a selection of interests chosen by the user in order to fit them with the ideal guide for their desired travel experience. Conducted user interviews to define interests and clarify design ideas.

USDA

Redesign of website for the U.S. Department of Agriculture with emphasis on modifying the information architect using card sorting technique in order to combine categories and create a new and more pragmatic sitemap. Defining the primary user was the key goal, which in turn led to the empathetic space required to design a practical experience for the user.
